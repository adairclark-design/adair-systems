# Adair Systems Business Card Concepts

## 01 / Signal Field

**Tagline:** Precision AI. Exceptional Software.

The most restrained concept. A single mint-to-purple signal line divides identity from contact, while the white logo badge creates one deliberate focal point. The back uses a large two-line promise, compact contact details, and a QR block isolated by negative space.

**Typography:** Helvetica Neue for identity and tagline; SF Mono for role, metadata, and contact details.

## 02 / System Blueprint

**Tagline:** Bespoke Systems. Intelligent by Design.

The most technical concept. A low-contrast construction grid and oversized ghosted logo turn the card into a miniature systems diagram. Condensed display type, capability indexing, and a framed QR zone create a precise engineering-instrument feel.

**Typography:** Avenir Next Condensed for display text; SF Mono for labels, coordinates, and contact details.

## 03 / Aurora Edge

**Tagline:** Engineering the Intelligent Advantage

The most cinematic concept. A razor-thin aurora edge frames a nearly empty front, while the back balances a cropped structural logo against an editorial tagline and compact contact column.

**Typography:** Helvetica Neue for editorial hierarchy; Menlo for title and contact details.

## Production Notes

- Finished artwork ratio: 3.5 x 2 inches at 300 DPI (1050 x 600 px).
- Recommended stock: 32 pt black duplex or soft-touch laminated 18 pt stock.
- Recommended finish: spot gloss on the logo and gradient rules; optional mint or purple edge paint.
- QR blocks are stylized placeholders and must be replaced with a production-tested QR code before printing.
